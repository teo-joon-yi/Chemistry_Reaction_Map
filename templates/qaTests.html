Additional Tests

Junction Test - React magnesium, manganese or zinc ions with ammonia to give precipitate. Then, add ammonium chloride, causing the precipitate to dissolve due to the shift in equilibria.

Copper Iodide Test - React copper (II) ions with potassium iodide. This forms a brown solution and a cream precipitate.

Sodium Carbonate Test - React any metal cation with a charge of 3+ with sodium carbonate, which will form carbon dioxide gas.
